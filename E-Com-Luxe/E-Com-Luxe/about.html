Our Story

In 2026, Luxe En Vogue was founded with a single conviction: well-made clothing should be accessible, and timelessness should never be sacrificed for trend.

Our philosophy

Clothing that earns its place

We believe that a thoughtfully designed wardrobe doesn't require constant refreshing. Each Luxe En Vogue piece is chosen for its ability to layer, to pair, and to look as refined in year two as it did on arrival.

  • Fabric sourcing prioritizes weight, hand-feel, and longevity over fast-fashion benchmarks.
  • Production runs are intentionally small, ensuring every piece receives attention to detail.
  • A singular palette — indigo and ivory — was chosen to eliminate decision fatigue and maximize mix-and-match potential.
  • We design for repair. Every seam is set to last decades, not seasons.

What we stand for

Our core values

01

Quality over Quantity

We measure success not by volume sold, but by the depth of connection our customers feel with each piece. Fewer items, owned longer, worn with intention.

02

Radical Honesty

Our care labels don't hide. Our pricing reflects real production cost. Our customer service operates on the principle that questions deserve clear, immediate answers.

03

Considered Design

Every colour, seam, and button placement is scrutinized. Nothing is added without reason. The result: pieces that feel intentional, never excessive.

Building Luxe En Vogue

Our journey

2026

Founded

Luxe En Vogue launches in Abeokuta then expands to Ibadan even though our services are not limited to those areas with a curated collection of essential pieces of fashion wears in indigo and ivory. The first 100 customers received personally handwritten thank-you notes.

Q1 2026

First Stock

Production expands from 6 to 24 pieces in the collection. Customer feedback drives the introduction of two new categories: knitwear and structured outerwear.

Q2 2026

Community Milestone

We reach 10,000 pieces shipped across West Africa. A dedicated customer care channel opens, responding to every inquiry within 24 hours.

Q3 2026

Tomorrow

We're working on something special — sustainability partnerships, bespoke tailoring services, and a loyalty program that rewards long-term wearers. More to come.
