Questions?

Frequently asked

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Don't hesitate to reach out to our team directly.

How long does shipping take?

+
Orders within Lagos are processed and shipped within 1–2 business days. Orders to other regions in Nigeria are delivered within 3–5 business days depending on location. We use reliable courier partners to ensure your pieces arrive safely.

What's your return and exchange policy?

+
We offer a 30-day return window on unworn items with original tags attached. If a size doesn't work, we'll arrange an exchange at no additional cost. Please contact our team at luxeenvogue1@gmail.com to initiate a return or exchange.

How should I care for my Luxe En Vogue piece?

+
All our pieces come with detailed care instructions on the label. Most items should be dry cleaned or gently hand-washed in cold water. We recommend air-drying to maintain the fabric integrity. For wool and cashmere, we suggest dry cleaning for longevity.

Do you offer custom tailoring or bespoke orders?

+
Yes! We're developing a bespoke tailoring service for custom pieces. Email luxeenvogue1@gmail.com with your specific requirements, and our design team will get back to you with timelines and pricing within 2 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and mobile money payments through our secure checkout. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.

How do I sign up for your newsletter?

+
You can subscribe to our newsletter from the homepage or footer of any page. Subscribers receive first access to new arrivals, exclusive styling tips, and occasional private sales. We send emails once per week and respect your inbox—unsubscribe anytime.

Is there a loyalty or rewards program?

+
We're currently building a loyalty program for our most valued customers. Early access will go to our newsletter subscribers. Stay tuned—you'll be among the first to know when it launches.
